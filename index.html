    Achievements

   Bravo!!! We Made It!

    AI-for-India 1.0 - The Guinness World Record Winning Event Broke the Record for most users taking an online computer programming lesson in 24 Hrs. GUVI, an Indian Institute of Technology-Madras incubated startup in association with AICTE & co-sponsored by BUDDI.AI, achieved a Guinness World Record through its ‘AI-for-India 1.0’ initiative. On 24th & 25th April 2021, GUVI delivered an Online Computer Programming lesson to around 6L users, leading to AI in 24 hours.

 Innovation Award 2021..!!

    The Sridevi Arunprakash Innovation Award is an annual award of ₹100,000 given to Women Innovators in the Edu-tech domain for a prototyped innovation or a commercialized product or service.This award presents an opportunity to foster & boost innovation among women for the benefit of anyone who wishes to learn & upgrade themselves with new skills through edu-tech services. The awardees will preferably be those women who believe not just in ‘implementing the best’ , but also in ‘focusing on the better’. Most importantly, it will value solutions that represent new pedagogical tools with 'innovation' yet 'affordable'.
